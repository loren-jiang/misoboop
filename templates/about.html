Mission

In creating this blog, I hope to bring a few key things to the dinner table:

  • Practicality

    If you’re looking for inspiration on wagyu beef recipes, this probably isn’t the place for you. For the most part, the recipes on this site will focus on ingredients and techniques readily accessible to the everyday home cook. There’s a time and occasion for extravagance, but I will try to share recipes that don’t require fancy kitchen accessories or ingredients.

  • Honesty

    Cooking is all about trial and error -- some things taste great, others not so much, but the key thing is I won’t lie to you that they do. Instead, I’ll do my best to iterate and improve.

  • American cooking with Chinese roots

    No, not literally ginseng… Raised on my mom’s cooking, I can assure you that Chinese dishes can sometimes be lackluster. The purpose of this blog isn’t to recreate authenticity, but rather to present Chinese dishes in a way that reflects the melting pot that is America.
